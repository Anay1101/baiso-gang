Meet the Baiso Gang

A quirky crew of chaos creators

🧠 Shreyan - The Mastermind

Role: Strategist and Chief Planner

About: Shreyan is the genius behind the gang’s most daring and ridiculous schemes. With his quick thinking and talent for convincing people that even the craziest ideas are foolproof, Shreyan keeps the gang two steps ahead (or two steps into trouble).

📍 Nanu - The Navigator

Role: Eternal Explorer

About: The irony is strong with Nanu, who, as the Navigator, is famous for getting lost in even the simplest of places. But her endless positivity and comedic charm turn any wrong turn into an adventure.

😂 Shreyas - The Jester

Role: Comic Relief and Mood Setter

About: Shreyas is the heart of the gang’s humor. Whether it’s a perfectly timed joke or a deliberately awful pun, he keeps spirits high and laughter constant in the gang.

🏆 Vedant - Former President

Role: Wisdom Provider

About: As a former member and president, Vedant’s legacy lives on. His sharp decision-making and inspirational speeches set the stage for what the gang is today. He’s missed, but his epic stories are still told with pride.

✨ Kanxini - The Spark

Role: Creative Dynamo

About: Kanxini’s creativity and unique ideas add sparkle to every plan. Her imaginative mind ensures no prank or scheme is ever dull.

🎨 Navkar - The Artist

Role: Visual Storyteller

About: Navkar captures the gang’s best moments with flair, turning chaos into beautiful memories. His artistic touch adds a special charm to their escapades.
